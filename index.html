welcome to virus.io

Play as a virus and get the highest score of each round. Each round lasts 5 minutes and the top 10 players (those with the highest number of points) are displayed on a scoreboard. How to play: Eat food (pellets) to increase your score and beat others by shooting at them, but make sure to avoid the sanitizer and the masks! Points are gained: - By eating pellets - By successfully killing other players Points are lost: - By colliding with sanitizer and mask - By shooting other players, this makes the player cautious of shooting randomly Health is lost: - When getting shot by the players - When colliding with sanitizer and mask Virus types (Players choose their virus): Blue Virus: Faster than the others, but loses double the points when hitting a mask/sanitizer. Red virus: Natural hunter, gains more points when it kills other viruses, but half points when eating food. Green virus: Strongest one, loses least points when hitting a mask/sanitizer but moves slower than the others.
